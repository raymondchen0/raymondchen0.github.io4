Calluna
Keep yourself and others safe, built in alerts and monitoring for seizures.
Branding & App Mockup
Overview
Calluna is a medical brand that focuses on improving the lives of those that suffer from epilispy. The goal is the give freedom back to those that suffer and ease their fear of experiencing a seizure and not being able to call for medical help.Calluna focuses on wearables that help detect a seizure and notify others if the wearer is experiencing one.

Audience & Research
Calluna was designed to help people that either suffer or know someone that suffers from epilepsy. I wanted to design an app and brand that would give people back their peace of mind. I focused on colors inspired by the epilepsy foundation while also keeping the overall mood calm.
