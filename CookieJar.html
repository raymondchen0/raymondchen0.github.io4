CookieJar
Regain your peace of mind. See how you can regain control of your valuable data.
Branding & Identity
Overview
CookieJar's goal is the inform the end user of how big companies enroach on and use their data. It does not block the user from using services that they have grown accustomed to. Instead, it simply warns and gives the user important information so that they can make an educated decision on how they want their data to be used.

Audience & Research
CookieJar evolved to have a more fun and playful tone. The topic of privacy and data is often scary so I wanted to create a brand that would not scare people away. However, as it further changed it became too playful for such a serious topic. In turn the design shifted and became darker in terms of imagery while maintaining an overall fun tone.
