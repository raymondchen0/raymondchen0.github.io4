Get a taste of these bold flavors. Oaks seasoning mix goes well on just about everything.
Packaging
Overview
Packaging / Product design for OAKS seasoning. This is my take on a brand that approaches its audience with a bold and loud attitude.
Audience
I wanted the brand to be known for its unique and bold flavors. I further emphasized the boldness by using a slab serif typeface in call caps. The dark background along with the bright colors also further emphasizes this point through the use of extreme contrast.
